Me

My corner of the web; for sharing code, ideas, and stuff that piques my interest.

The site

Is designed to be a personal dashboard that maintains itself. The contents of the portfolio are pulled directly from Github and similarly the blog from my obsidian notes.

This allows me to maintain a steady flow when working. Refining my notes as I go and tagging any polished articles as published.

As for the technology used;

  • ~ HTML
  • ~ CSS
  • ~ vanilla JS
  • ~ C
  • ~ Bash

It's static and lightweight, going bare bones, with no bloat. I wanted the snappy, no flicker feel of an SPA like application but without the overhead of external dependencies.
